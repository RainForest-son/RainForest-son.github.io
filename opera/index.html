Huangmei Opera

1. Abstract

Huangmei Opera is one of China's intangible cultural heritage, which has a history of more than 200 years. In recent years, the singing synthesis technology based on deep learning has been rapidly developed and can synthesize high quality songs, but such methods require a large amount of high quality audio training data. This paper constructs a singing data set of Huangmei Opera, and formulates a data annotation method suitable for Huangmei Opera. The data set contains the score information of the music, all the audio is sampled at a high rate of 44100kHz, and the audio data set is marked and annotated with phonemes, phoneme duration, and note and word boundaries. To demonstrate the validity of the dataset, we constructed a singing synthesis model based on a deep learning model and evaluated the model by using the subjective mean opinion score MOS indicator and an objective indicator. The experimental results show that the proposed model is suitable for the synthesis of the singing voice of Huangmei Opera.
